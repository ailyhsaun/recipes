Dry:

 - 6 Cups pretzel sticks
 - 6 Cups Cheerios
 - 7 Cups Shreddies

Seasoning: 

 - 1 Cup butter
 - 4 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
 - 1.5 tsp seasoning salt
 - 1 tsp garlic salt
 - 1 tsp onion salt
 - .5 tsp celery salt
 - 2 tsp brown sugar

Melt butter and seasoning ingredients together.

Pour and stir the seasoning mixture over roasting pan 
of dry ingredients. 

Cook at 225 for 20 minutes, stirring halfway through.
